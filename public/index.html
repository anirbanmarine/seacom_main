About Us

Our Aim

We aim to offer the best infrastructure for maritime training in Eastern India.

Our History

Over the past few decades we have trained thousands of seafarers to serve the maritime industry. Today they serve the world’s fleet and achieved positions of prominence on their own diligence.

Our Promise

We promise to provide the best training programs for novice and seasoned seafarers. Assist the novice seafarers in finding the berths in merchant vessels.

World Class Infrastructure with Impeccable Pedagogy

We offer the best infrastructure in Marine Colleges of Eastren India. Let us get you started on your career at Sea.

Courses Offered

General Purpose Crew

A course designed for the crew of todays merchant ships , ready to take the challenges of tomorrow.Course Duration-6 months.

Bachelor of Nautical Science

For the Officers and Masters of the world’s ships. Course Duration-3 years. Candidates selected by the shipping companies are welcome.

Electro Technical Officer

For an Exciting career as an Electro Technical Officer on board the ships of the Merchant Navy. Course Duration-4 months

ECDIS(STCW)

A Post-Sea course for Electronic Chart Display Information System. This is essential for aspiring Second Mates. Course Duration 5 days

STSDSD(STCW)

A Post-Sea course for Security Training for Seafarers with Designated Security Duties. A mandatory Course for All Seafarers.

AFF(STCW)

A Post -Sea course for Advanced Fire Fighting . A mandatory course for all support level officers.
