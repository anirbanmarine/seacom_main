About Us

Our Aim

We aim to offer the best infrastructure for maritime training in Eastern India.

Our History

Over the past few decades we have trained thousands of seafarers to serve the maritime industry. Today they serve the world’s fleet and achieved positions of prominence on their own diligence.

Our Promise

We promise to provide the best training programs for novice and seasoned seafarers. Assist the novice seafarers in finding the berths in merchant vessels.

World Class Infrastructure with Impeccable Pedagogy

We offer the best infrastructure in Marine Colleges of Eastren India. Let us get you started on your career at Sea.
